Admission to the AME program at Awadh Aero DAC Aviation Academy is conducted through an Entrance Examination process, ensuring selection in accordance with institute guidelines and DGCA regulatory standards.

1

Eligibility Criteria

  • a) Candidates must have completed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry & Mathematics (PCM) or an equivalent qualification recognized by the Government of India.
  • b) Applicants must meet the required medical fitness standards as per aviation norms.
2

Registration

  • a) The Free Registration Form can be filled up online and submitted along with the required academic details within the prescribed timeline.
3

Filling of Application Form

  • a) Application form can be filled & submitted online/hard copy along with the fee of ₹500/-
4

Counselling

  • a) Candidates will be invited for counselling and document verification at the Institute.
5

Final Admission

  • a) Admission will be confirmed after successful verification of documents and completion of institute formalities at AADAA Campus Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh.
