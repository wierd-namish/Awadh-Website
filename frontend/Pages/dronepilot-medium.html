Medium Class Drone Training

Medium class UAVs represent the cutting edge of industrial farming, heavy surveying, payload delivery, and security patrols. They require advanced knowledge of telemetry loops, flight dynamics, and payload sensors.

Our specialized program covers dual-link communications, autonomous waypoint grids, and liquid payload dynamics (for agricultural sprayers). Practical flight testing occurs in clear regional fly zones.

Eligibility

Class 10th Pass, Class 2 Medical

Duration

10 Days (Includes Simulators)

Heavy-Lift Agriculture Drone

Specialized Curriculum

Module 1: Autopilot & Grids

Mapping mission grids, configuring obstacle avoidance sensors, and managing flight control software (Mission Planner/QGroundControl).

Module 2: Payload Operations

Calibrating spray pumps, nozzle pressures, handling multispectral imaging sensors, and cargo release mechanism locks.

Module 3: Crew Coordination

Pilot-in-Command duties, visual observer communication, checklist flows, and emergency battery-fail protocols.
