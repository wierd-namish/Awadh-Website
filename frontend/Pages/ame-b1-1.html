Category B1.1

Aeroplane Turbine

Category B1.1 (Aeroplane Turbine) is a specialized aircraft maintenance engineering certification focused on turbine-powered airplanes. Individuals holding this certification are qualified to maintain, repair, overhaul, and troubleshoot various aircraft systems, including engines, structure, hydraulics, flight controls, and landing gear.

At Awadh Aviation Academy, students receive hands-on training in turbine engine systems, aircraft maintenance, hydraulics, airframe inspection, and safety procedures, preparing them for successful careers in the aviation maintenance industry.

Eligibility

10+2 with Physics, Chemistry & Math

or

Diploma in Mechanical, Electrical, or Electronics & Aeronautical Engineering

Duration

2 Years (4 Semesters)
