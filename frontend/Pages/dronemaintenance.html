UAV Assembly & Repair

Maintaining flightworthiness in commercial drone fleets is essential. Our course targets structural soldering, Electronic Speed Controller (ESC) wiring, compass calibration, and IMU sensor debug loops.

Students build a full quadcopter frame from scratch, configure and compile flight firmware, and perform pre-flight stress tests to ensure reliable and safe outdoor flight performance.

Eligibility

Class 10th / 12th Pass or Equivalent

Duration

4 Weeks (Certificate Course)

Electronics Repair Bench

Course Chapters

Chapter 1: Hardware & Structure

Carbon fiber frame load limits, landing gear systems, brushless motors selection, and propeller balancing techniques.

Chapter 2: Power Loops & Solder

LiPo battery safety, power distribution board soldering, ESC wiring, motor direction tests, and telemetry modules connectivity.

Chapter 3: Firmware & Calibration

Flashing flight controllers (ArduPilot/Betaflight), setting PID loops, accelerometer calibration, and fail-safe transmitter testing.
