Small Class Drone Pilot Course

Small class drones (from 2kg up to 25kg) are widely used for photography, construction surveys, infrastructure inspection, and regional crop monitoring.

Our certification program integrates multi-rotor aerodynamics, emergency landing routines, and navigation logs. Students practice on high-fidelity UAV simulators before moving to our open flight fields.

Eligibility

Class 10th Pass, 18+ Years Old

Duration

5 Days (Intensive Program)

UAV Flying Zone

Program Modules

Day 1 & 2: Theory & Simulators

Basic aeronautics, weather boundaries, airspace regulations, and 5+ hours of flight simulation inside our computer labs.

Day 3 & 4: Live Flight Training

Takeoffs, landings, figure-eight maneuvers, waypoint flying, GPS tracking, and return-to-home emergency tests.

Day 5: Evaluation & Certification

Practical flying test, logging protocols review, and certificate generation aligning with commercial drone standards.
