Who We Are

Awadh Aero Adventures Association is a registered non-profit organization dedicated to revolutionizing India's aerial adventure and aviation landscape. Based in Mankapur, Uttar Pradesh, the Association offers certified training programs in high-flying pursuits—from paragliding and power hang gliding to microlight flying—alongside cutting-edge drone technology and unmanned aviation training. With a commitment to safety, skill development, and structured ecosystem building, Awadh Aero combines exhilarating recreational flying experiences with professional aeronautical education. Through workshops, awareness camps, and strategic collaborations with government and industry partners, the Association empowers youth, adventure enthusiasts, and aspiring aviators while fostering regional tourism and employment opportunities in the aviation sector.

Our Vision

To become a pioneering institution for providing accessible, safe, and professionally managed training and adventure experiences through powered hang gliding and UAS ecosystem.

Our Mission

To promote and popularize powered hang gliding and other aero-adventure activities. To develop skilled aero-sport professionals and aviation enthusiasts, especially among youth in rural and semi-urban areas. To establish a UAS Ecosystem for drone pilot training, MRO, R & D and manufacturing of affordable drones. To partner with educational, governmental and tourism bodies for integrated aviation awareness and adventure promotion.

About Awadh Aero Dac Aviation Academy

aadaa is a next-generation, state-of-the-art Aircraft Maintenance Engineering (AME) training institute built to support India's rapidly growing aviation industry.

The academy focuses on developing highly skilled aviation professionals through industry-oriented training, digital transformation, and AI-powered learning methodologies.

Its objective is to bridge the growing gap between India's aviation industry's demand for qualified aircraft engineers, technicians, and aviation support professionals by providing practical, hands-on training aligned with current industry standards.
